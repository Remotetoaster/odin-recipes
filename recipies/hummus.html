Homemade Hummus

Surpisingly quick and easy hummus recipie

Ingredients

  • Sesame Seeds
  • Olive Oil
  • Chickpeas
  • Salt
  • Smoked Paprika
  • Cumin
  • Lemon Juice

Steps

  1. Boil (or slow cook) dried chickpeas for at least 1hr 20 mins.
  2. Toast sesame seeds on a frying pan till brown.
  3. Puree toasted seeds and olive oil -- with salt to taste. Once satisfied with consistency, you can stop and store as Tahini!
  4. Add chickpeas, cumin, lemon juice, and paprika.
  5. Add salt if too flavourless, chickpeas if too liquidy, olive oil if dry.
