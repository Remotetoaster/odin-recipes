Homemade Gnocci

Suprisingly easy and tasty pasta recipie that can be made within an hour

Ingredients

  • Russet Potatoes
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Peel then boil potatoes for at least 1hr -- then mash.
  2. Add egg(s) and flour to food processor and knead.
  3. Coat work surface with flour and work till less sticky.
  4. Divide into ropes, cut into bite-sized pillows, press with fork.
  5. Boil pieces untill they float.
