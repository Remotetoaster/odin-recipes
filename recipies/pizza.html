Homemade Pizza

Surprisingly easy homemade pizza that's better than the restaurant

Dough

  • Olive Oil
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Active Dry Yeast

Neapoliton

  • Spicy Pizza Sauce
  • Fresh Mozza/Boccicini
  • Pizza Mozza
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Basil
  • Cornmeal

Blac

  • Old Cheddar
  • Feta
  • Garlic Salt
  • Margerine
  • Riccota
  • Jalepenos

Steps

  1. Pour flour, salt, yeast, olive oil, and water into the large food processor and knead twice.
  2. Coat mixing bowl with olive oil and cover for minimum 1hr 30mins
  3. Coat surface with flour and mix dough with hands. Cut dough in half and cover both for 15mins.
  4. Place pizza stone in oven and start timer for 30 mins at 500 degrees.
  5. Flatten out both doughs and coat with olive oil -- let stand for 15 mins.
  6. Coat larger dough with pizza sauce, mozza, bocconcini, basil, and italian seasoning.
  7. Place on stone for 10mins -- Let stand for 5 to 8 mins.

Steps (Blac)

  1. After placing stone in oven, nuke margerine and garlic salt for 45sec.
  2. Coat surface with garlic spread, shredded old cheddar, feta, riccota, and jalepenos.
  3. After the neapoliton is done, place the blac on the stone for 10 mins.
